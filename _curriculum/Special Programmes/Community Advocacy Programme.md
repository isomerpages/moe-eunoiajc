<h4><em>“I alone cannot change the world, but I can cast a stone<br>across the waters to create many ripples.”<br></em><strong><em>Mother Teresa</em></strong></h4>
<p>The Community Advocacy Programme (CAP) seeks to grow Thinkers with Heart
and aims to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Build greater awareness on the importance of understanding real community
issues and its impacts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Internalise core values of community through experiential learning and
deep introspection</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advocate for a particular social cause</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide a platform to embark on community outreach to meet the needs of
the local community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>As part of CAP, you will have the opportunity to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Identify a social cause you believe in</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do thorough research on an identified social cause</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advocate for the cause with a group of interested students</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create awareness through community outreach (within and outside of school)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Execute community outreach</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inspire like-minded students to continue advocacy efforts for the identified
social cause</p>
</li>
</ul>
