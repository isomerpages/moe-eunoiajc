<p>We
<br><strong>Enable</strong>&nbsp;students to learn continuously,
<br><strong>Empower</strong>&nbsp;students to seek all-round excellence, and
<br><strong>Embolden</strong>&nbsp;students to pursue their purpose.</p>
<h3><strong>Values</strong></h3>
<p>We live with integrity;
<br>truthful to self and to others.</p>
<p>We connect with love;
<br>love transcends borders.</p>
<p>We lead courageously;
<br>courage brings us forward.</p>
<p>We serve selflessly;
<br>our joy is in giving.</p>
<h3><strong>Student Outcomes</strong></h3>
<p>Eunoians are critical and inventive thinkers
<br>who have strong moral and intellectual integrity.
<br>They are anchored in values,
<br>guided by purpose
<br>and fuelled by passion.</p>
<p>They are perceptive youth
<br>who respect ideas and embrace diversity.
<br>They give the best of themselves
<br>and bring out the best in others.</p>
<p>They are skilful and courageous leaders
<br>who inspire hope and excellence.
<br>They are unafraid to stand up for what is right,
<br>even if it means standing alone.</p>
<p>They are concerned citizens and global ambassadors
<br>who serve others humbly and selflessly.
<br>They contribute actively to society
<br>and strive to advance Singapore
<br>and the world.</p>
