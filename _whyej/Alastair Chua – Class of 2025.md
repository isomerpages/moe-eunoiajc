<p><strong>Alastair Chua</strong> (24-E1) approached his EJ journey with quiet
strength and steady leadership. Known for his thoughtfulness and perseverance,
he consistently tackled challenges with maturity while forging meaningful
connections with both peers and teachers. Alastair’s empathy and genuine
care for others have left a lasting mark on those around him, inspiring
respect and admiration throughout the community.</p>
<p>“My journey in EJ has been one of growth, resilience, and finding a sense
of belonging in unexpected ways. Coming from the far west and being the
only one from my JAE school, I initially found the environment unfamiliar
and intimidating. However, over time, what once felt distant gradually
became warm and comforting. This is a place I now call home.</p>
<p>What shaped my experience most were the people I met along the way. I
was constantly inspired by individuals who were hardworking, kind, and
passionate, and they taught me that it is not the “grade of the pencil,
but the line it leaves” that truly matters. These encounters have left
a lasting impact on who I am today.</p>
<p>Amid the challenges of the A-Level season, I also learned the importance
of being kind to myself, a lesson that grounded me during stressful moments.
Beyond academics, it is the simple, everyday experiences that I cherish
most, such as sharing meals with friends, walking to lectures together,
and finding joy in the ordinary.</p>
<p>Looking back, EJ has given me more than just an education; it has given
me friendships, perspective, and a place where I truly feel at home.”</p>
