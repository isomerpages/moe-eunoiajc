<p>I am <strong>Ethan</strong>  <strong>Teoh</strong> from 24-I5, and I led the
EJC Floorball team as Captain. EJC will always hold a special place in
my heart as it was a place that allowed me to strive towards excellence,
to overcome adversities and also a place to enjoy myself. Juggling studies
and Floorball was definitely one of the hardest challenges I faced, however,
this defined my EJ journey and made it so memorable.</p>
<p>During my time in EJ, there were many challenges I had to face, but there
were always the people in the background who truly inspired me and helped
me to push through. My classmates, teachers, teammates and even coaches
were always there to provide the necessary support in any way that they
could. For example, when I went overseas for competitions, my classmates
and teachers would help me to keep up with the lessons I had missed, so
that I wouldn't be left behind.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The 2025 A Division games would be the most memorable experience for me
as I led my team, alongside my vice-captain, to strive for the best. Although
we did not get the results that we initially aimed to achieve, getting
to the finals with the team that I had was a far more memorable experience,
one that I would truly never forget.</p>
