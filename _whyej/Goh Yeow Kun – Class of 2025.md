<p><strong>Goh Yeow Kun</strong>, Vice-Chairperson of 24-I5, was a key part
of the team that set up the People of Eunoia initiative, which seeks to
celebrate the unsung heroes of EJC and their stories.</p>
<p>“Despite having little prior leadership experience, EJ provided me with
many opportunities for growth, such as being a part of the Programme Committee
for Orientation '25, managing the Instagram page for the Class Directorate,
and being a Subject Specialist in Project AEGIS.&nbsp;</p>
<p>My two-year journey in EJ has been a very fruitful and fun one, and I
will deeply miss my time in EJ. It was the people around me who motivated
me the most. Whenever a challenge popped up, my friends, teachers, and
classmates were always there for me. There was always someone I could rely
on — someone there to help me, encourage me, and push me on.</p>
<p>Orientation ’25 is definitely my most memorable experience. During both
the planning phase and the event, we shared so much laughter and had so
much fun together — I dare say we had just as much fun as the incoming
J1s! I still remember waking up on the Saturday after Orientation ended,
asking myself what activities were planned for the day, only to realise
it was all over.”</p>
