<p>Co-Curricular Activities are an integral part of education in EJC. How
else will you discover your interests and talents?</p>
<p>Taking up a CCA helps you understand yourself as you lead yourself and
your peers towards excellence. You will also meet&nbsp;your best friends
as you learn, play and grow together with them. Those who reach the stars
must first reach inward.</p>
